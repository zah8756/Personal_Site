Chatboard

My masters project

Figma

This project aimed to conduct a usability study on a classroom-focused alternative to Slack and Discord, which I named Chatboard. Fearing that I did not have time to create the program, I made a mockup for user testing in Figma.

The research study was conducted on college students at RIT using Figma's prototyping tool and Google Forms to gather data.
